## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**How do I restore my Premium purchase?**
If you got a new phone or reinstalled the app, you can easily restore your purchase. Open Aquacall, navigate to the Premium upgrade screen, and tap **"Restore Purchases"** at the bottom. You will not be charged again.

**Can I track beverages other than water?**
Aquacall Premium allows you to track custom beverages like coffee, tea, and soda, adjusting your hydration goals accordingly. 

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
You can manage or cancel your Aquacall Premium subscription directly through your Apple ID settings. Go to the Settings app on your iPhone > Tap your Name > Subscriptions > Aquacall.
