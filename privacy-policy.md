# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the AquaCall mobile application (“the App”).

## Information We Collect

### Health Data
With your permission, the App may read and write hydration data to Apple Health using HealthKit. This may include:

- Water intake records
- Hydration-related health information

This data is accessed only after you grant permission through Apple Health.

### Usage Data
The App may collect limited anonymous usage data such as:

- App interactions
- Feature usage

This helps improve the performance and functionality of the App.

### Device Information
Basic device information such as device type and operating system version may be collected for debugging and performance optimization.

## How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used only to:

- Track your hydration progress
- Display water intake statistics
- Improve app performance and user experience

Your health data is never used for advertising purposes.

## Data Storage

Your data may be stored:

- Locally on your device
- In your personal iCloud account (if cloud sync is enabled)

The developer does not have access to your personal health data stored on your device or iCloud.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or health data with third parties.

Health data accessed via HealthKit is used only for the purposes described in this policy and in accordance with Apple's guidelines.

## Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for analytics or diagnostics. These services may collect anonymous technical information to help improve app performance.

## Your Privacy Rights

You can manage or revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through:

Settings → Privacy → Health → Apps

You may also delete your app data by uninstalling the App.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
